Software Developer Requirements

Required education: High School Diploma and at least a Bachelor's in Computer Science or Software Engineering.

Significant math requirements are completed in these programs such as calculus, differential equations, linear algebra and physics.

Skills required: Knowledgeability in at least 1 development software language such as C, C++, Java or Python.

Experience is not mandatory but can be a good factor when applying for a position as a software developer.
