What do Software Engineers do?

Creating Software

Executing and creating advanced and real-life applicable programs using C++, Python, JavaScript, HTML and other languages

Testing Programs

Manage, test , regulate and troubleshoot software to maximize profitability

Designing and Planning

Designing, developing and writing software systems with scientific and mathematical models

Preparing Reports

Analyzing information to manage software + hardware systems

Why are Software Engineers Important?

Developent & Maintenance

Hence the name, Software Engineers are important to keep computers developed and maintained by designing and implementing new programs + features

Security

Develop and maintain computer systems and ensure the privacy and information of users secured

Improving Sales & Services

Engineers create well-designed programs and online platforms that facilitate access clients or customers
